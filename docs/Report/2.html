APEX's feature


-HERO & MAP

APEX has 19 heros, and they have their own unique skills.

APEX has four maps, every map has different size and landmark; therefore, you should take different strategy at different map. It will significantly help you easier to win.


-Guns & SKINS

Every heros and guns have special skins. The skins don't effect gun's damage and accuracy, and don't effect hero's speed. But you will be happy when you have that cool stuff.

Like other games, APEX have Sniper, Pisto, Rifle and other guns. Try every gun and find the gun that fits you.
