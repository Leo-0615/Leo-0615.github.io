How to play


-MOVEMENT

Use WASD to control your character to move.

Use CTRL to slide,which is a useful tip to move faster.

Use SHIFT to run. Although you can move faster, enemy can hear your sound clearly.


-HEALING

Every character in APEX have health point and shield. If you get hurt, you need to heal.

Use battery to recovery your shield.

Use med-kit to recovery your health point.


-SHOOTING

You can find guns on the ground or boxes, use them to fight with others.

Shooting is the most important skill in APEX. Aimming at the enemy is the essential point to win the game.
