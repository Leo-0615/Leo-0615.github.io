GLOBAL COMPETITION


-ALGS

Although APEX was launched less than 3years, there is a lot of people addicited to APEX. APEX has already gain huge popularity. Not suprisingly, holding a competition is a must.

ALGS is the global competition held by EA(APEX development company). The pinnacle of the ALGS is "ALGS championship". Only 40 teams can get the ticket to the championship. Players must get enough points in previous "qualifiers" and "playoffs" to participate in the game.

Professional teams participate in ALGS not only want to fighting for glory, but also want to win the prize money. The total prize is 5,000,000USD, and it has successfully attracted people from all over the world to join. It is really a significant competition in the world.
